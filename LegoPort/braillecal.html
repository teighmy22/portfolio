About the Project:


For this projet we had to create an abstract graphic system tht builds on the rhythm of a unique representation of time.

Final print in the laser cutter

I found inspiration from a font I found in research called Braille Neue. This accessible font is able to match the braille up with letters in the alphabet making it possible for the visually impaired to understand the text as well. The challenge was how I could get the font to work if touched.

After creating my design in Illustrator I took it into Rhino 7 where I prepped it for the laser cutter. During this process I had to match up the holes that I wanted to cut out with my design. The final print ended up taking about twenty minutes per page.
